# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants should keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- using clear, evidence-based technical arguments;
- giving constructive feedback with enough context to act on it;
- accepting responsibility for mistakes and correcting them;
- respecting different levels of experience and availability.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks;
- discriminatory language or conduct;
- publishing private information without explicit permission;
- sustained disruption of technical discussion;
- bad-faith security disclosures or exploit publication before maintainers have a reasonable chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict participation, or block users when behavior materially harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository's private maintainer channels. Do not include unrelated private information in public issues.
